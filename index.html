A place to begin

Life can feel heavy when you are trying to manage everything alone.

You may be feeling anxious, stuck, overwhelmed, disconnected, or unsure why certain situations affect you so deeply.

Therapy offers space to slow things down, understand yourself more clearly, and build a more compassionate relationship with your inner world.

Anxiety and overwhelm

Relationships

Exploring patterns, boundaries, attachment, conflict, loneliness, and connection.

Self-understanding

Making sense of your story, your emotions, and the parts of you that feel hidden.

How therapy works

Gentle, honest, collaborative.

My approach is relational and reflective. We work together to understand what feels difficult, what has shaped you, and what may help you move forward.

You do not need to arrive with everything figured out. We can begin with what feels most present now.
