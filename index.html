Feeling overwhelmed, stuck in your thoughts, or not quite like yourself anymore?

A calm, supportive space to slow down, make sense of what you are carrying, and begin to feel more like yourself again.

A gentle place to begin

Starting therapy can feel like a big step. You don’t need to have everything figured out before you arrive.

My role is not to fix you, but to walk alongside you — helping you understand yourself, feel heard, and find your own way forward.

I may be able to help with

  • Overthinking and feeling unable to switch off
  • Emotional overwhelm or burnout
  • Low self-worth or harsh self-criticism
  • Feeling stuck, lost, or disconnected

How I work

My approach is grounded, relational, and person-centred. I offer a space where you can speak freely, without judgement or pressure.

Together, we gently explore what’s going on beneath the surface — at a pace that feels right for you.

I am currently training as an integrative counsellor, with a strong foundation in the person-centred approach. My work is guided by empathy, honesty, and respect.
