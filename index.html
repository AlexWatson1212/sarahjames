A gentle place to begin

Reaching out for therapy can feel like a big step, especially when life already feels heavy. My aim is to offer a warm, steady space where you do not have to have everything worked out before you arrive.

I may be able to help with

  • Anxiety and constant overthinking
  • Feeling overwhelmed or emotionally exhausted
  • Low self-worth or harsh self-criticism
  • Life changes, loss, or feeling stuck

How I work

My approach is warm, relational, and thoughtful. I work at a pace that feels manageable, helping you explore what is going on beneath the surface without pressure or overwhelm.

Therapy is not about having the right words. It is about having a space where you can arrive honestly, be met with care, and begin to understand yourself more clearly.

Practical details

I offer online sessions and in-person sessions in Manchester.
