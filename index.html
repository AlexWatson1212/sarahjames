A gentle place to begin

Reaching out for therapy can feel like a big step, especially when life already feels heavy. You do not need to have everything worked out before you arrive.

My aim is to offer a warm, steady space where you can speak honestly, feel less alone, and begin to make sense of what you have been carrying.

I may be able to help with

  • Anxiety, overthinking, and feeling unable to switch off
  • Emotional overwhelm, burnout, or exhaustion
  • Low self-worth, shame, or harsh self-criticism
  • Life changes, loss, relationship difficulties, or feeling stuck

How I work

My approach is warm, relational, and thoughtful. I work at a pace that feels manageable, helping you explore what is happening beneath the surface without pressure or overwhelm.

Therapy is not about having the right words. It is about having a space where you can arrive honestly, be met with care, and begin to understand yourself more clearly.

I work with empathy, honesty, and respect. The relationship between therapist and client is central to my work, and I aim to offer a space that feels safe, steady, and human.
